Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Job Opportunity 2026 ("we", "our", "us") is a job-listing directory that links to official company career pages. This policy explains what information we collect and how it's used when you visit this site.

Information we collect

This site's core browsing and search features run entirely in your browser — searching, filtering, and viewing job listings does not send your queries to a server. If you use the Contact form, we collect the name, email, and message you voluntarily provide, solely to respond to your inquiry.

Third-party career pages

Every "Apply Now" button on this site links to a third-party company's official careers website. Once you leave our site, that company's own privacy policy and terms apply. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you submit on those external sites.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Standard browser session data may be used only for basic functionality like remembering search filters during your visit.

Accuracy of listings

We make reasonable efforts to link only to genuine, official company career pages. However, job postings and career page URLs are controlled entirely by third-party employers and may change without notice. We recommend always verifying a role directly on the employer's site before applying or sharing personal information.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for job seekers and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@jobopportunity2026.com or via our Contact page.
